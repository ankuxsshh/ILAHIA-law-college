Follow these simple steps to apply for our LLB programs.

3-year Unitary LLB:

  • Must have a Bachelor's degree with a minimum of [X%] marks.

2. Entrance Exam (if applicable)

Admission is based on [mention the entrance exam, e.g., CLAT, AILET, or College-specific exam].

3. How to Apply
  • Obtain the application form from the Admission Office.
  • Fill out the form and submit required documents.
  • Pay the application fee via [cash/demand draft/bank transfer].

Bring original documents for verification, including mark sheets, degree certificates, and ID proof.

Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat in the program after document verification.
